## Disambiguation

- **drive-motivation vs staff-engineer:** drive-motivation is about *designing* motivation systems (for teams or product). staff-engineer is about *operating* as a Staff-plus IC who happens to need motivation literacy. Use drive-motivation when designing incentives; staff-engineer when navigating the role.
- **37signals-way vs staff-engineer:** 37signals-way is operating-model for *running a team or company* (cycles, async, scoping). staff-engineer is operating-model for an *individual contributor* working across teams. They compose: a Tech-Lead-archetype Staff engineer in a 37signals-style org needs both.
